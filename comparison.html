üéØ Summary & Recommendations

  • Choose Atomic Nutrition Tracker if: You want a completely free, modern, AI-powered tracker with unique features like macro-aware AI suggestions and meal completion. Perfect for tech-savvy users who value open-source software.
  • Choose MacroFactor if: You want the most scientifically advanced TDEE tracking and automatic macro adjustments. Best for serious bodybuilders and athletes willing to pay for cutting-edge algorithms. Most affordable premium option at $6-12/month.
  • Choose MyFitnessPal if: You need extensive third-party integrations (200+ apps), community features, and the world's largest food database (14M+ foods). Good free tier available, but many features require Premium ($80-100/year).
